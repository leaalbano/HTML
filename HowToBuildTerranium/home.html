This is a closed water terranium

What is it?

                                              Closed terraniums are supposed to be self-sustaining. This means that
                                              organisms work together in order to keep a functioning and balanced 
                                              ecosystem. In a freshwater ecosystem, it is common to consist of plants
                                              and feeder organisms. An example of this could be aquatic snails and algae.
                                              The snails feed on algae in order to prevent overgrowth in the container.
